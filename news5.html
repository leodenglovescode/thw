Mac Hagen Created a COMPANY!

2021/12/6 Mon




Mac Hagen opened a company that sells and produces masks. The company is called "Mask 'n Masks" According to MaskPharmacy.com they are having a bad time selling masks because Mac Hagen's company produces masks so rapidly that everyone in the world can buy enough for themselves. "Mac uses a technique called the APST (Atoms Produce Speedily Technique). This technique is only available to scientists, but we don't even know how he got this!" MaskPharmacy.com said. Though the technique situation continues to be a mystery, Mac Hagen is dominating the whole world with masks. "Covid can be defeated by masks! Buy a mask at masknmasks.net today!" Said Mac Hagen. A few days later, Mac Hagen's factory was struck by lightning. That caused the machines to malfunction. Masks began to fill up the APST machine. The machine then exhausted all of the masks out from the chimney. "Gosh! It's raining Mask 'n Masks!" Mr. McFatty said to the interviewer. "What the heck?" exclaimed a man passing by the factory. A few hours later, the masks factory of Mask 'n Masks around the world all malfunctions, causing the world to be covered by masks. "I guess we don't have to buy masks for a very long time," commented Mr. Bigface. Police and the military are trying to solve the tremendous mask problem.
